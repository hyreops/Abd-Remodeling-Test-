About ABC Remodeling

Quiet craftsmanship, built on fifteen years of trust.

We started ABC Remodeling with a simple idea: a kitchen is not a product, and it shouldn't be sold like one. It's the room where families build their days, and it deserves the same care a homeowner would give it themselves.

Over 15 years and more than 100 completed projects, we've built a practice rooted in St. Louis — working with the city's older homes, its newer builds, and the people who love both.

Every kitchen we touch is led by the same team from first sketch to final walkthrough. No subcontracted promises. No surprises.

Frequently asked

Answers, before you have to ask.

How long does a typical kitchen renovation take? +
Most full kitchen renovations run between 8 and 14 weeks from demolition to walkthrough. Design and material lead times add roughly 4 to 8 weeks before construction begins. We'll give you a firm calendar after the design phase, not a guess.
What does a kitchen renovation cost in St. Louis? +
Our renovations typically range from $65,000 to well over $200,000 depending on scope, cabinetry, and finishes. We provide a detailed, line-item proposal after the consultation so you can make informed decisions — no vague allowances.
Do you handle design and construction, or just one? +
Both. We're a design-build practice, which means the same team that draws your kitchen also builds it. That eliminates the gap between intent and execution — and a lot of the cost overruns that come with it.
Are you licensed and insured? +
Yes. We carry full general liability and workers' compensation coverage and pull every permit required for the work. We're happy to share certificates and references on request.
Can I live in my home during the renovation? +
Most clients do. We set up dust barriers, protect adjacent spaces, and help you plan a temporary kitchen setup. That said, some larger renovations — especially those involving structural work — are smoother if you're able to be out for a few weeks.
What neighborhoods do you serve? +
We work throughout the St. Louis metropolitan area — Clayton, Ladue, Webster Groves, Kirkwood, the Central West End, Lafayette Square, and surrounding communities. If you're in the region, get in touch.
What kind of warranty do you offer? +
A two-year workmanship warranty on everything we install, plus manufacturer warranties on cabinetry, hardware, appliances, and finishes. If something isn't right, we come back and make it right.
